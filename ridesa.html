RIDESA

Interuniversity Network for the Development of the Sugar-Energy Industry

Ridesa is the network that supports our study in sugarcane breeding program at Federal University of Vi√ßosa. For this, we can count on laboratories, field experiments, and technical support to develop our research studies.
Varieties of acronym RB, developed by RIDESA, are being cultivated in more than 65% of the area with sugarcane in the country. It contributes to about 12.3% in the energy matrix of Brazil. For these new varieties to be developed, RIDESA has 79 research bases, encompassing University Laboratories, Crossing Stations, Experimental Stations, and Selection Bases, the latter being conducted in partnership with companies in the sugarcane sector.
Also, I have the opportunity to follow other experiments during the data collection and the investigative process. It is also a place where we study alternative technologies to improve both investigative and cultivation operations.
